About Duck Power

So, what is Duck Power? We are a company that takes everyday cars that are bound by the limits of the pavemnet and remove those limits. Just as a duck is not limited to walking, flying or swimming, why should our cars?

Now we can't literally make your car fly, swim, or walk, but we can do this in other ways. For flying, we want to make it so we can jump our cars and not destroy everything when they land. For swimming, we want to make it so our cars can go beyond the pavement, make it so they can see dirt roads, see the mud and water. For walking, we mean your car is still capable of being a car, street legal. So when you pull up to a traffic light or pass them on the highway, they are stunned by your quacking abilities.
